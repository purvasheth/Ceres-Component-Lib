Introduction

Its important to understand how layouts can be managed easily using this library. There are a few predefined helper classes for layouts, margin and paddings.

Layouts

Padding Classes

  • p-1 --> 1em padding
  • p-2 --> 2em padding
  • p-3--> 3em padding
  • pt-1 --> 1em padding top
  • pb-1 --> 1em padding bottom
  • pl-1 --> 1em padding left
  • pr-1--> 1em padding right

Margin Classes

  • m-1 --> 1rem margin
  • m-2 --> 2rem margin
  • m-3 --> 3rem margin
  • mt-1 --> 1rem margin top
  • mb-1 --> 1rem margin bottom
  • ml-1 --> 1rem margin left
  • mr-1 --> 1rem margin right
  • mt-2 --> 2rem margin top
  • mb-2 --> 2rem margin bottom
  • ml-2 --> 2rem margin left
  • mr-2 --> 2rem margin right
